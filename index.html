About Me

Making Maths Click for Every Student

Hi 👋, I’m TK Maile — a dedicated mathematics educator who believes that every student is capable of understanding and loving mathematics. My approach is warm, patient, and grounded in real understanding rather than just memorising formulas.

I teach online classes that are interactive, structured, and tailored to each student’s pace. Whether you are struggling with basics or pushing towards advanced topics, there’s a place for you in my classroom.
